How to make Mango Lassi

Description

Lassi come in all kinds of flavors, some are salty, some are sweet, some have mint, some have fruit. A mango lassi is basically a yogurt-based mango milkshake or smoothie.

Lassi are an ancient drink originating from the Punjab region (Northern India and Eastern Pakistan). A lassi is a blended drink with yogurt, water, spices and sometimes fruit.


Ingredients

  • 1 cup of extra ripe chopped mango
  • 1 cup of plain yoghurt
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 4 teaspoons of honey
  • Ground cardamom
  • Ice
  • Your Choice of Garnish

Steps

  1. Put the mango, yogurt, milk, honey (or sugar), and cardamom (optional) into a blender and blend for 2 minutes. If you want a thicker consistency, either blend in some ice as well or serve over ice cubes.

  2. Pour contents into a glass and sprinkle with a tiny pinch of ground cardamom to serve.


Note: The lassi can be kept refrigerated for up to 24 hours.
