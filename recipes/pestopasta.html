How to make Pesto Pasta

Description

This top-rated pesto pasta recipe, which comes together in just 15 minutes, is the perfect quick and easy weeknight dinner. Pesto is a paste made of crushed garlic, pine nuts, salt, basil leaves, grated cheese such as Parmesan or pecorino sardo, and olive oil.

Pesto pasta is easy to make and full of flavor. It tastes good hot or cold! It's also perfect for all occasions! Given that it is easy to make, and most importantly, it tastes delicious!


Ingredients

  • Pasta (whatever shape)
  • Sauteed Onion
  • Pesto Sauce
  • Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water and drain.

  2. Cook the onion in oil, then stir in the pesto and seasonings.

  3. Add the pesto mixture to the hot pasta and toss with cheese.
