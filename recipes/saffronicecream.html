How to make Saffron Ice Cream

Description

Known as Bastani, Saffron Ice Cream may be one of the most delicious desserts from Iran! It has a similar taste to roce ice cream and has a very distinct taste.

Saffron Ice Cream is also very simple and easy to make, meaning it can be the perfect dessert for any occasion. There are many variations of Bastani, however in this recipe I will provide the most simple and healthy version of the dessert.


Ingredients

  • Saffron
  • Rose Water
  • White Sugar
  • Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Cream
  • Chopped Pistachio
  • Your Choice of Garnish

Steps

  1. Take store bought ice cream out of the freezer and allow it to soften in a large bowl, but not become fully melted

  2. Pour in the infused saffron water, add rose water and gently mix through using a spatula or wooden spoon until combined

  3. Add most of the chopped pistachios and fold to combine, leaving some on the side for garnish

  4. Chop up the frozen cream into tiny pieces

  5. Pour the ice cream mixture back into the same ice cream container and add frozen cream pieces. Fold gently into the ice cream

  6. Garnish with extra pistachios, rose petals and place back in the freezer to set for approximately 8 hours or overnight. Serve Persian Ice Cream in bowls or in between circle wafers to create saffron ice cream sandwiches and enjoy!!!
